##### The ingredients needed to make Lasagna Neapolitan:

1. Make ready 28 oz spaghetti sauce or 1 recipe of Martha Stewart spaghetti sauce bolognese
1. Get 1 lb ground beef (If using canned spaghetti sauce)
1. Get 12-14 green or regular lasagna noodles
1. Take 1 cup Mozzarella cheese
1. Make ready 1 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
1. Prepare 2 cup Milk
1. Make ready 1/4 cup Flour
1. Make ready 1/4 cup Butter
1. Get 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
1. Get 1 pinch Ground nutmeg
1. Prepare to taste Salt n pepper




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Lasagna Neapolitan:

1. Cook lasagna noodles until al dente about 8 minutes
1. Spread out on oiled cookie sheets (not overlapping) and cover with tea towel to keep them from drying out
1. Grease a 13 by 9 inch baking dish
1. If using canned spaghetti sauce- cook the ground beef until no longer pink
1. Drain any fat from the ground beef; season with salt and pepper, oregano and garlic powder (or 1tsp Italian seasoning)
1. Add spaghetti sauce to meat and set aside
1. To make the white sauce; in a medium saucepan melt the butter over medium heat
1. Add the flour and cook stirring constantly for one minute
1. Gradually stir in milk and cook gently until thickened
1. Remove from heat and add 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese; nutmeg, salt and pepper to taste
1. To assemble lasagna spread some meat sauce and white sauce at the bottom of the lasagna pan and cover with a layer of noodles
1. Using a large spoon, put some meat sauce then white sauce over each noodle and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese
1. Repeat the layers using all ingredients until all have been used, ending with white sauce
1. Top with mozzarella and dust with Parmesan cheese
1. Bake in a preheated 350°F oven 50 to 60 minutes
