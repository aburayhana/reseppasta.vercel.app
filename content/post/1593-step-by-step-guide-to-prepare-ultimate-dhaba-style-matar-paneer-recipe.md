##### Instructions to make Dhaba Style Matar Paneer Recipe:

1. Heat a pan and add 2 tbsp oil
1. Add black pepper &amp; Clove
1. Add onion and fry 2 mins.
1. Add green chilli, Kashmiri. red chilli &amp; kaju and mix well.
1. Add tomato &amp; salt &amp; mix well
1. Cover and cook for 5 to 7 mins.
1. Mix well &amp; cool completely and grind.
1. Heat a pan and add oil 3 tbsp.
1. Add paneer and fry 2 mins.
1. After 2 mins remove paneer
1. At jeera in Same pan
1. And add dry garam masala &amp; fry ½ min.
1. Add dhaniya powder, Haldi &amp; red chilli powder.
1. Immediately add paste.
1. And mix well.
1. And add ½ cup water.
1. Cover and cook 2 mins.
1. Add matar and cover and cook 8 to 10 mins.
1. Add kasuri meethi and garam masala mix well.
1. Add paneer and mix well.
1. Cover and cook only 2 mins.
1. Ready matar paneer add coriander leaves.
