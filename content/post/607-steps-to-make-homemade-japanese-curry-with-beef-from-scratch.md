##### Instructions to make Japanese Curry with Beef (from scratch):

1. Heat 2 Tbsp of cooking oil in a wok until hot and dry the onions until it is fragrant and translucent. Then add the apples and caramelise them for 2-3 minutes.
1. Then add the beef, mix well and cook them until the beef is brown all over. Then add in the potatoes, Celery, carrots and stir fry them for 1 minutes. Add stock/water as well as 1 Tbsp of curry powder and let it cook until veggies and beef are tender.
1. Meanwhile, in another wok, and in bitter and flour, mix well and cook them to a smooth consistency (try not to let the butter become brown). Add the S&amp;B Curry Powder and Katsu sauce and mix well. Lastly add the stock and mix it to smooth consistency again.
1. Add the Curry Roux to the Beef and Veggie broth and then mix well. Season with Salt and then serve.
